## Suggesting New Cafes

Know a great matcha cafe that's not on MatchaMap? Help us expand the map!

### How to Submit a Suggestion

1. **Access Suggestions**
   - Click **Menu** (☰)
   - Select **Suggest a Cafe**

2. **Fill in Cafe Details**

**Required Information:**
- **Cafe Name:** Official name
- **Full Address:** Street address with city and postal code
- **City:** Select from available cities (Toronto, Montreal, etc.)

**Optional Information:**
- **Neighborhood:** Local area name (e.g., "Kensington Market")
- **Description:** Why you love this cafe (max 1000 characters)
  - What makes their matcha special?
  - Atmosphere and ambiance
  - Unique features
  - Personal experiences
- **Google Maps Link:** Direct link to the cafe
- **Instagram Handle:** Their Instagram (@username)
- **Website:** Cafe website URL

3. **Submit for Review**
   - Click **Submit Suggestion**
   - Our team will review within 7 days
   - You'll be notified of the decision

### What Happens Next?

**Pending Review:**
- Your suggestion appears in your profile under "My Suggestions"
- Status: **Pending**
- Admin team reviews the submission

**Approved:**
- Cafe is added to MatchaMap
- Status changes to **Approved**
- You're credited as the suggester (optional)
- You can visit and review the cafe!

**Rejected:**
- Admin provides reason in notes
- Common reasons:
  - Cafe already exists in database
  - Cafe doesn't serve matcha
  - Location outside current coverage areas
  - Incomplete or inaccurate information
- You can resubmit with corrections

### Tips for Good Suggestions

1. **Do Your Research**
   - ✅ Search MatchaMap first to avoid duplicates
   - ✅ Verify the cafe serves matcha (not just green tea)
   - ✅ Check if they're still open

2. **Provide Complete Information**
   - ✅ Full address with postal code
   - ✅ Accurate Google Maps link
   - ✅ Current social media handles
   - ✅ Detailed description

3. **Add Context**
   - ✅ Explain what makes this cafe special
   - ✅ Mention specific drinks to try
   - ✅ Note if it's a hidden gem
   - ✅ Share your experience

4. **Be Accurate**
   - ✅ Double-check spelling
   - ✅ Verify address and links
   - ✅ Use official cafe name
   - ✅ Confirm they serve authentic matcha

### Tracking Your Suggestions

**View Submission History:**
1. Go to **Your Profile**
2. Click **Suggestions Tab**
3. See all your submissions

**Status Indicators:**
- 🟡 **Pending:** Under review
- 🟢 **Approved:** Added to map
- 🔴 **Rejected:** Not added (see admin notes)

## Leaderboards

Compete with the community and discover the most active matcha enthusiasts!

### Passport Leaderboard

**What It Ranks:** Total cafes visited (check-ins)

**How to Climb:**
- Visit more cafes
- Check in at each location
- Complete your Matcha Passport

**View Leaderboard:**
- Main Menu → Leaderboards → Passport
- Filter by:
  - **Time Period:** All-time or Monthly
  - **City:** Toronto, Montreal, etc. or All Cities
- See top 50 users

**Your Rank:**
- View your current position
- See cafes visited count
- Track passport completion %

**Strategy Tips:**
- 📍 Visit new cafes regularly
- 📍 Check in at every visit
- 📍 Explore different neighborhoods
- 📍 Aim for 100% passport completion

---

### Reviewer Leaderboard

**What It Ranks:** Total reviews + reputation score

**How to Climb:**
- Write detailed reviews
- Get "helpful" votes from others
- Upload photos with reviews
- Provide valuable insights

**Ranking Formula:**
- Primary: Number of reviews written
- Secondary: Reputation score (from helpful votes)

**View Leaderboard:**
- Main Menu → Leaderboards → Reviewers
- Filter by time period and city
- See top 50 reviewers

**Your Rank:**
- View your current position
- See total reviews written
- Check reputation score
- View total photos uploaded

**Strategy Tips:**
- ✍️ Write comprehensive reviews
- ✍️ Include photos
- ✍️ Be honest and helpful
- ✍️ Explain what makes cafes special
- ✍️ Engage with other reviews

### Contributor Leaderboard

**What It Ranks:** Combined contributions

**Contribution Score:**

**How to Climb:**
- Contribute across all categories
- Write reviews
- Upload photos
- Curate favorites

**View Leaderboard:**
- Main Menu → Leaderboards → Contributors
- Filter by time period and city
- See top 50 contributors

**Your Rank:**
- View your current position
- See breakdown of contributions
- Track total contribution score

**Strategy Tips:**
- 🎯 Balance all contribution types
- 🎯 Quality over quantity
- 🎯 Stay active regularly
- 🎯 Engage with community

---

### Leaderboard Features

**Time Periods:**
- **All-Time:** Total contributions since joining
- **Monthly:** Current month's activity (resets each month)

**City Filters:**
- View leaders in your city
- Compare across cities
- See regional differences

**Privacy:**
- Only public profiles appear
- Private users don't rank
- Toggle in Privacy Settings

**Updates:**
- Rankings refresh every 5 minutes
- Monthly leaderboards reset on 1st of month
- All-time tracks lifetime stats

## FAQ

**Q: How long does cafe suggestion review take?**
A: Usually 3-7 days. Complex cases may take longer.

**Q: Why was my suggestion rejected?**
A: Check admin notes in your Suggestions tab for specific reasons.

**Q: Can I edit a pending suggestion?**
A: Not currently, but you can submit a new one with corrections.

**Q: Do I get credit for approved suggestions?**
A: Yes! Approved suggestions show your username (if you opt-in).

**Q: How often do leaderboards update?**
A: Every 5 minutes for real-time rankings.

**Q: Can I filter leaderboards by friend?**
A: Not yet, but friend/follower leaderboards are planned.

**Q: Do private profiles appear in leaderboards?**
A: No, only public profiles are ranked.

**Q: How is reputation score calculated?**
A: Based on "helpful" votes on your reviews and overall engagement.
