## What are Custom Lists?

Custom lists let you organize cafes into personalized collections:

**Examples:**
- "Best Cafes for Work" - Quiet spots with wifi
- "Weekend Brunch Spots" - Cafes with food and ambiance
- "Must-Try in Toronto" - Your top recommendations
- "Hidden Gems" - Underrated cafes worth visiting
- "Date Night Cafes" - Romantic atmosphere spots

**Features:**
- ✅ Create unlimited lists
- ✅ Add notes to each cafe in a list
- ✅ Public or private visibility
- ✅ Share lists with friends
- ✅ View lists from other users

## Creating Lists

### Step 1: Access Lists

**From Main Menu:**
1. Click **Menu** (☰)
2. Select **My Lists**
3. Click **Create New List** (+)

**From Your Profile:**
1. Go to **Your Profile**
2. Click **Lists Tab**
3. Click **Create New List** (+)

### Step 2: Fill in List Details

**Required Fields:**
- **Name:** Short, descriptive title (max 100 characters)
  - Good: "Best Matcha Lattes in Toronto"
  - Bad: "Cafes" (too generic)

**Optional Fields:**
- **Description:** Explain what makes this list special (max 500 characters)
  - Example: "My go-to spots for creamy, authentic matcha lattes. Perfect for matcha purists!"

- **Visibility:**
  - **Public:** Anyone can see and follow this list
  - **Private:** Only you can see this list (default)

### Step 3: Save and Start Adding

- Click **Create List**
- You'll be taken to your empty list
- Start adding cafes!

## Adding Cafes to Lists

### Method 1: From a Cafe Page

1. Visit any cafe page
2. Click **Add to List** (📋)
3. Select the list(s) to add to
4. Optionally add notes (e.g., "Try the iced latte")
5. Click **Add**

### Method 2: From Your List

1. Open your list
2. Click **Add Cafes** (+)
3. Search or browse cafes
4. Select cafes to add
5. Add optional notes for each
6. Click **Add Selected**

### Adding Notes to List Items

Notes help you remember why you added a cafe:

**Examples:**
- "Best latte art in the city"
- "Order the ceremonial grade matcha"
- "Go on weekday mornings - less crowded"
- "Ask for oat milk - it's the best"

**Tips:**
- Keep notes short and actionable
- Include specific drink recommendations
- Mention best times to visit
- Note any special instructions

## Managing Your Lists

### Editing Lists

**Update Name or Description:**
1. Open the list
2. Click **Edit List** (✏️)
3. Update fields
4. Click **Save**

**Change Visibility:**
1. Open the list
2. Click **Settings** (⚙️)
3. Toggle **Public/Private**
4. Click **Save**

### Reordering Cafes

*Coming Soon* - Drag and drop to reorder cafes in your list

### Removing Cafes

1. Open the list
2. Find the cafe to remove
3. Click **Remove** (×) next to the cafe
4. Confirm removal

### Deleting Lists

1. Open the list
2. Click **Settings** (⚙️)
3. Scroll to **Danger Zone**
4. Click **Delete List**
5. Confirm deletion

**⚠️ Warning:** Deleting a list is permanent and removes all items!

## Sharing Lists

### Make a List Public

1. Open the list
2. Click **Settings** (⚙️)
3. Toggle **Public**
4. Click **Save**
5. Your list is now discoverable!

### Share List URL

1. Open a public list
2. Click **Share** (🔗)
3. Copy the link
4. Share via:
   - Social media
   - Messaging apps
   - Email

**Example URL:** `https://matchamap.app/lists/123-best-cafes-toronto`

### Discover Public Lists

1. Visit any user's profile

## Best Practices

### Creating Great Lists

1. **Be Specific**
   - ❌ "Good Cafes"
   - ✅ "Best Matcha Lattes with Oat Milk"

2. **Add Descriptions**
   - Explain the theme or purpose
   - Help others understand your criteria
   - Share your expertise

3. **Include Notes**
   - Add value with specific tips
   - Note what to order
   - Mention best times to visit

4. **Keep Lists Focused**
   - 5-15 cafes is ideal
   - Too many = overwhelming
   - Too few = not useful

5. **Update Regularly**
   - Add new discoveries
   - Remove closed cafes
   - Refine notes based on visits

### Sharing Guidelines

- 🌟 Make lists public if you want to help others
- 🌟 Use clear, descriptive names
- 🌟 Add a description explaining your criteria
- 🌟 Credit others if you were inspired by their list
- 🌟 Respond to comments and questions (coming soon)

## FAQ

**Q: How many lists can I create?**
A: Unlimited! Create as many as you need.

**Q: Can I add the same cafe to multiple lists?**
A: Yes! A cafe can be in as many lists as you want.

**Q: What happens if a cafe closes?**
A: We'll notify you, and you can remove it from your lists.

**Q: Can I collaborate on lists with friends?**
A: Not yet, but collaborative lists are planned for the future!

**Q: How do I make my list popular?**
A: Share it on social media, make it public, add great descriptions, and keep it updated.

**Q: Can I export my list?**
A: Not yet, but export functionality is planned (CSV, PDF, etc.)

**Q: Do list creators get notified when someone views their list?**
A: Not currently, but analytics may be added in the future.
