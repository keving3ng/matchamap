## Following Users

### How to Follow Someone

1. **Find a user** through:
   - Reviews on cafe pages
   - Comments on reviews
   - Leaderboards
   - Search (if implemented)

2. **Visit their profile** by clicking their username

3. **Click the "Follow" button** on their profile

4. **You're now following!** You'll see their activity in your feed (coming soon)

### View Your Following List

- Go to **Your Profile → Following Tab**
- See everyone you're following
- Unfollow users by clicking "Unfollow"

### View Your Followers

- Go to **Your Profile → Followers Tab**
- See who's following you
- View their profiles

### Following Etiquette

- ✅ Follow users whose taste aligns with yours
- ✅ Engage with their reviews through comments
- ✅ Discover new cafes through their check-ins
- ❌ Don't spam follow/unfollow repeatedly

## User Profiles

### Your Public Profile

Your profile showcases your matcha journey:

**Profile Information:**
- Display name
- Bio (max 500 characters)
- Location
- Profile picture
- Social links (Instagram, TikTok, website)

**Activity Stats:**
- Total reviews written
- Total check-ins
- Total photos uploaded
- Passport completion %
- Reputation score
- Follower count
- Following count

**Badges:**
- Achievements earned (Explorer, Reviewer, Photographer, etc.)
- Badge progress indicators

### Viewing Other Profiles

When you visit another user's profile, you can see:

- Their basic information (if public)
- Their reviews and ratings
- Cafes they've visited (check-ins)
- Photos they've uploaded
- Badges they've earned
- Their followers and following (if not hidden)

**Note:** Users can set their profile to private, hiding their activity from non-followers.

## Leaderboards

Compete with the community on three leaderboards:

### Passport Leaderboard

**Criteria:** Total cafes visited (check-ins)

- See who has explored the most cafes
- Track your progress toward the top
- Filter by city to see local leaders
- View monthly leaders vs. all-time

**Access:** Main Menu → Leaderboards → Passport

### Reviewer Leaderboard

**Criteria:** Total reviews written + reputation score

- Recognize the most helpful reviewers
- Reputation increases with helpful votes
- Quality over quantity matters
- Filter by city and time period

**Access:** Main Menu → Leaderboards → Reviewers

### Contributor Leaderboard

**Criteria:** Combined contributions (reviews + photos + favorites)

- Celebrate the most active community members
- All contributions count equally
- Encourages diverse participation
- Shows total contribution score

**Access:** Main Menu → Leaderboards → Contributors

### Your Rank

Check your rank on any leaderboard:
- View your current position
- See stats that contribute to your rank
- Set goals to climb the rankings
- Compare yourself to friends

## Privacy Settings

Control what others can see about your activity.

### Profile Privacy

**Public Profile (Default):**
- ✅ Anyone can see your profile
- ✅ Appears in leaderboards
- ✅ Reviews and photos visible to all
- ✅ Followers/following lists visible

**Private Profile:**
- ❌ Only followers can see your profile
- ❌ Not listed in leaderboards
- ❌ Reviews and photos only visible to followers
- ❌ Follower count hidden

**To change:** Profile → Settings → Privacy → Public/Private

### Activity Visibility

Control specific aspects of your profile:

- **Show Activity:** Display check-ins and recent actions
- **Show Followers:** Let others see who follows you
- **Show Following:** Let others see who you follow

**To change:** Profile → Settings → Privacy → Activity Visibility

### Best Practices

- 🔒 **Start public** to build community connections
- 🔒 **Go private** if you prefer selective sharing
- 🔒 **Customize visibility** for granular control
- 🔒 **Review regularly** as your preferences change

## FAQ

**Q: Can I unfollow someone?**
A: Yes! Go to their profile or your Following list and click "Unfollow."

**Q: Will users be notified when I follow them?**
A: Currently no, but notifications may be added in the future.

**Q: How is reputation score calculated?**
A: Based on helpful votes on your reviews and overall engagement.

**Q: Can I block users?**
A: Not yet, but this feature is planned.

**Q: How do I appear in leaderboards?**
A: Your profile must be public, and you need activity (check-ins, reviews, etc.)

**Q: Can I hide specific reviews or photos?**
A: Yes, you can delete individual reviews or photos, or set your entire profile to private.
