## What is MatchaMap?

MatchaMap is your guide to discovering exceptional matcha cafes. We combine:

- **Expert Reviews** - Detailed reviews with scores for matcha quality, ambiance, and value
- **Community Ratings** - Real user reviews and photos
- **Interactive Map** - Find cafes near you with distance and directions
- **Matcha Passport** - Track your cafe visits and earn badges
- **Social Features** - Follow other matcha lovers, share your experiences

## Creating Your Account

### Why Create an Account?

With a free account, you can:
- ✅ Write reviews and upload photos
- ✅ Check in to cafes you visit (Matcha Passport)
- ✅ Save your favorite cafes
- ✅ Follow other users
- ✅ Earn badges and achievements
- ✅ See personalized recommendations

### Sign Up Steps

1. **Visit MatchaMap**
   - Go to [https://matchamap.app](https://matchamap.app)

2. **Click "Sign Up"**
   - Located in the top right corner

3. **Enter Your Details**
   - **Email:** Your email address (kept private)
   - **Username:** Choose a unique username (visible to others)
   - **Password:** Minimum 8 characters

4. **Create Account**
   - Click "Create Account"
   - You'll be redirected to the login page

5. **Log In**
   - Enter your email and password
   - Click "Log In"

**Note:** Email verification is currently optional but recommended for account security.

## Exploring Cafes

### Using the Map View

The interactive map shows all cafes in your selected city.

**Features:**
- **Cafe Pins** - Each pin represents a cafe
- **Click a Pin** - View cafe quick info in a popup
- **Pan & Zoom** - Drag to move, scroll to zoom
- **Your Location** - Blue dot shows your current location (if enabled)

**Finding Cafes Near You:**

1. **Enable Location Access**
   - Click "Use My Location" button
   - Allow location access in your browser

2. **View Distance**
   - Cafes now show distance from you
   - "10 min walk" appears on cafe cards

3. **Get Directions**
   - Click "Get Directions" on any cafe
   - Opens in Google Maps

### Using the List View

Switch to list view for a detailed overview.

**Sorting Options:**
- **By Distance** - Nearest cafes first (requires location)
- **By Rating** - Highest rated cafes first
- **By Neighborhood** - Grouped by area
- **By Name** - Alphabetical order

**Filtering:**
- **Minimum Score** - Show only 8+ rated cafes
- **City** - Switch between Toronto, Montreal, Tokyo, etc.
- **Search** - Search by cafe name or neighborhood

### Viewing Cafe Details

Click any cafe to see the full details:

- **Expert Review** - Detailed review by our team
- **Drink Scores** - Ratings for specific drinks (0-10 scale)
- **User Reviews** - Community reviews and ratings
- **Photos** - User-uploaded photos
- **Location** - Address and map
- **Hours** - Opening hours (if available)
- **Social Links** - Instagram, TikTok links
- **Pricing** - Typical prices and alt milk charges

## Your First Check-In

The **Matcha Passport** lets you track cafes you've visited.

### How to Check In

1. **Visit a Cafe**
   - Actually visit the cafe in person!

2. **Open MatchaMap**
   - Navigate to the cafe's detail page

3. **Click "Check In"**
   - Button appears on cafe detail page
   - Only available when logged in

4. **Add Notes (Optional)**
   - Add private notes about your visit
   - Example: "Tried the ceremonial grade latte - amazing!"

5. **Submit**
   - Your check-in is saved
   - Cafe appears in your Passport
   - Progress towards badges updated

**Check-In Benefits:**
- ✅ Track your matcha journey
- ✅ Earn completion badges
- ✅ See your passport completion percentage
- ✅ Private notes for each visit
- ✅ Share your progress with friends

## Writing Reviews

Share your experience with the community!

### Your First Review

1. **Visit Cafe Page**
   - Go to the cafe you want to review

2. **Click "Write a Review"**
   - Located below expert review
   - Must be logged in

3. **Rate the Cafe**
   - **Overall Rating** (required) - Your overall experience (0-10)
   - **Matcha Quality** (optional) - Quality of the matcha
   - **Ambiance** (optional) - Atmosphere and decor
   - **Service** (optional) - Staff friendliness and speed
   - **Value** (optional) - Price vs quality

4. **Write Your Review**
   - **Title** (optional) - Short headline
   - **Review Text** (required) - Describe your experience
   - Minimum 50 characters recommended

5. **Add Details**
   - **Tags** - Add tags like "creamy", "authentic", "Instagram-worthy"
   - **Visit Date** (optional) - When you visited
   - **Visibility** - Public (visible to all) or Private (just for you)

6. **Upload Photos (Optional)**
   - Add up to 5 photos
   - Max 10MB per photo
   - JPEG, PNG, or WebP format

7. **Submit Review**
   - Click "Post Review"
   - Review goes live immediately
   - Photos require admin approval before showing publicly

### Review Guidelines

**Do:**
- ✅ Be honest and constructive
- ✅ Describe specific drinks you tried
- ✅ Mention standout details (ceremony, foam art, etc.)
- ✅ Note the vibe and atmosphere
- ✅ Upload clear, well-lit photos

**Don't:**
- ❌ Post spam or promotional content
- ❌ Use offensive language
- ❌ Leave fake reviews
- ❌ Upload photos of people without consent
- ❌ Copy reviews from other sites

**Note:** You can only leave one review per cafe. You can edit or delete your review anytime.

## Using the Matcha Passport

Your Passport tracks your matcha journey across all cafes.

### Accessing Your Passport

1. **Click Your Profile Icon**

2. **Select "Matcha Passport"**

3. **See Your Progress**

### Passport Features

- Visual map of all cafes you've visited

## Customizing Your Profile

Make your profile your own!

### Profile Settings

1. **Click Your Profile Icon**
   - Top right corner

2. **Select "Settings"**
   - Edit your profile information

3. **Update Your Profile**
   - **Display Name** - How others see you (optional)
   - **Bio** - Tell others about yourself
   - **Location** - Your city (optional)
   - **Avatar** - Upload a profile photo (future)

4. **Add Social Links**
   - **Instagram** - Your Instagram handle
   - **TikTok** - Your TikTok handle
   - **Website** - Your blog or website

5. **Set Preferences**
   - **Favorite Style** - Hot, Iced, or Blended
   - **Dietary Restrictions** - Vegan, Dairy-free, etc.

6. **Privacy Settings**
   - **Public Profile** - Make your profile visible to everyone
   - **Show Activity** - Display your recent reviews and check-ins
   - **Show Followers** - Display who follows you

### Privacy Options

**Public Profile (Default)**
- Anyone can view your profile
- Your reviews are public
- Your passport is visible

**Private Profile**
- Only followers can see your profile
- Your reviews are still visible (but not linked to profile)
- Your passport is hidden

**Activity Settings**
- Choose what appears on your profile
- Control who sees your check-ins
- Manage follower visibility
